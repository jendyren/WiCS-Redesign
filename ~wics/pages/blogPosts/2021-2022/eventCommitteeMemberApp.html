WiCS is Looking for New Event Committee Members!




WiCS is looking for one new event committee member (any academic standing) and one new freshman event
committee member (freshman academic standing) to serve for the 2021-2022 academic year!!

In total, this election will be for two new event committee members.
The responsibilities of an event committee member include coordinating and planning WiCS
events and collaborations. As an e-board member, you will also be able to attend our family (e-board) meetings
and provide your input on future WiCS activities.

NOTE: Please send a video with your speech (2-3 minutes) about why you would
like to be a WiCS event committee member!
Elections will be done virtually.

Active members will be able to view your video and vote anonymously.

If you'd like to run for Event Committee Member, please fill out the form and send in your speech by Saturday, October 30th at 11:59 PM!
