WiCSter of the Month

We're excited to introduce our October WiCSter of the Month, Nicole Niemiec!

Nicole is a senior double majoring in Computer Science and Applied Math with a minor in History. She is a part of University Scholars, secretary of Tau Beta Pi Honor Society, a member of WUSB, and works on the PoliTech VIP Team. In her free time, Nicole enjoys playing and listening to music including hosting a radio show at our radio station WUSB, going to concerts, and spending time with her friends and family.
Favorite programming language: Python
Favorite movie(s): Speed and Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron
