HackHealth is a health-themed hackathon that welcomes all students and is devoted to being inclusive and beginner-friendly.

Hosted annually by Women in Computer Science at Stony Brook University

February 12 - 13, 2021 • Virtual Hackathon

Register Volunteer Become a Mentor

What

A 24 hour health-themed hackathon devoted to being inclusive and beginner-friendly, providing a comfortable environment for everyone to develop their ideas and improve their skills.

Who

HackHealth welcomes all current university students, regardless of major or gender. Open to coders and designers of all skill levels.

Where

Virtual on the HackHealth Discord Server

When

February 12th - February 13th
Friday, 5PM - Saturday, 8PM
