Apply to the WiCS Mentoring Program!

Monday - September 13, 2021

Hey WiCSters! 

New to Stony Brook? Not quite sure what being a CS major is all about? Interested in learning about what it's like working in industry? Or just need someone to talk with or ask questions to? 

Join the WiCS Mentoring Program! 

The mentoring program is designed to help students learn more about computer science at Stony Brook, and become more involved in the WiCS community! Mentors will help answer questions from mentees and provide guidance. 

New this year: WiCS alumni will also be mentors! For those upperclassmen wanting to get a summer internship or transition into full time you know what to do! 

Sign up as a mentee or mentor here: http://bit.ly/3DCxZGV ! Feel free to dm us or email wics@cs.stonybrook.edu if you have any questions!
